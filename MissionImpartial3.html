Free/Affordable Mental Health Resources

Unfortunately, mental health resources and information on how and where to receive treatment for mental illness is not easily accessible to everyone. One of the objectives of this project, is to create a platform containing the relevant resources and information necessary to seek treatment for mental illness and to learn more about the importance of mental health!
