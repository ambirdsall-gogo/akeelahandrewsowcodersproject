About The Creator of Mission Impartial

Welcome to Mission Impartial! I’m Akeelah Andrew, and I’m currently a business student living in the Caribbean. Advocating for social justice has been a passion of mine for a very long time now. I’ve decided to dedicate this project to mental health advocacy with a focus on the effects of oppression on the mental health of oppressed people. Over the past few years of being an activist I’ve learnt (and I’m still learning) so much related to this topic and others. I hope to share some of what I’ve learnt with you on this platform :)

About Mission Impartial:

Mission: To Address Social Issues by Educating and Advocating Through Media.

Mission Impartial is a website that discusses the impact of oppression on the mental health of marginalized communities. It is also a platform containing a compilation of free and affordable mental health resources. The main objective of Mission Impartial, is to encourage conversations pertaining to how oppression affects mental health, as well as to provide adequate resources to aid further research and seeking affordable treatment for mental illness.
